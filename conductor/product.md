# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A comprehensive fitness management platform designed to streamline the interaction between personal trainers and their students. It facilitates workout planning, progress tracking, and communication, ensuring a structured and efficient path to fitness goals.

## Target Audience
- **Personal Trainers:** Professionals managing multiple clients, creating workout plans, and monitoring progress.
- **Students/Clients:** Individuals following assigned workout routines and tracking their fitness journey.

## Core Features
- **Authentication:** Secure login for both trainers and students.
- **Role-Based Access:** Distinct dashboards and views for trainers and students.
- **Trainer Dashboard:** Overview of students, ability to assign workouts, and view student progress.
- **Student Portal:** View assigned workouts, log completion, and track personal stats.
- **Progress Tracking:** Visualizations of workout history and achievements.
- **Profile Management:** Personal details and settings for all users.
